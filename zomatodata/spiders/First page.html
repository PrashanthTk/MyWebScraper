          Need I say anything, the name talks for itself.
        Having lived in Hyderabad  for an year and Paradise being my favorite dine-in places, I was overjoyed on seeing the place come up at OMR. Was one of the first crowds to dine in here during the launch and already have made a few luncheons here.
        It is known that Biriyani here, is out of the world! But kebabs and curries really steal the thunder here. No place in the city comes close to the Garlic, Kalmi and Hara bhara kebabs served here.
        Paradise Special Chicken curry, Chicken Kandhari and Chicken Nawabi are definite must haves here.

        However amazing their consistency in maintaining the taste of Biriyani, sadly Double ka meetha and Qubani ka meetha seem to be a challenge for folks here. Have found these items undo an excellent meal more than once. I hope the team rolls their sleeve up on this. 

        Rest Assured 5.0 Rating!!!
